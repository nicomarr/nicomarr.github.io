Hi there! I'm Nico Marr, and this is my personal website. 
 
I'm an independent researcher and educator with a background in immunology, microbiology, virology, human genetics, and systems biology. These days, much of my time goes into teaching, and into learning from the open-source community, with a current focus on integrating LLMs and agentic coding tools into biomedical research workflows for exploring data and generating insights. This work supports researchers and community organizations in resource-limited settings, a mission shared with [Theme Bio](https://themebio.org), a U.S. 501(c)(3) organization I co-founded.
 
I maintain this website to share things I'm working on. Here you can find links to all my [publications]({{ '/publications/' | relative_url }}), [tutorials]({{ '/tutorials/' | relative_url }}), [code]({{ '/code/' | relative_url }}), [courses]({{ 'en/teaching/' | relative_url }}) I'm developing and teaching, and other resources.
 
To learn more about me, check out my [About]({{ '/about/' | relative_url }}) page.
